About the event

The biggest and best hardware event is here at RAGHU EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS . Make-a-thon is an 24-hour hardware-software mixed event where participants come together to create something amazing! Make-a-thon is an event where participants get to show their technical skills within a limited amount of time. This is a chance to create awesome projects from scratch!

How's it going to be?

The participants must create a prototype of their ideas using only the provided components. The participants are not encouraged to bring any electronic materials or components. All the general equipment will be provided here. A theme will be disclosed at the time of the event and the participants must build their creation related to the theme. All projects must use some hardware(minimum requirements must be met).Whether it is analog, digital or embedded is up to you.

  • Registration fee : 500/-per head(if individual participation) 1000/-per team(max 3 members).
  • Prize money:
  • 1st prize 10,000/-
  • 2nd prize 5,000/-
  • All the participans will be given certificate of participation
  • All the campus ambassador's will be given certificate of appreciation

RULES

  • The team must not exceed a maximum of 3 members.
  • The project must be completed within the specified time of 24 hours
  • The team should bring their own laptops and other tools.(Basic software will be provided here)
  • Net facility will be provided if necessary.
  • The team must be responsible in case of any damage for the provided equipments.
  • Standard components for the project will be provided at the venue. If the team needs any customized components they need to specify it before hand
  • As long as you are a student registered to an institution(college), you are allowed to participate.
  • The participants need to register online and the payment should be on spot.
  • The team mates can be of any college.
  • 3D printing and two layer PCB design facilities are available. But the participants must bare the cost of printing and manufacture.
  • The participants must mention their requriments through mail by sending the component list to the specified E-mail address, if the components they require are not mentioned in the hardware list prior 4 days in advance.
  • The participants are requested to specify their hardware platform (arduino or raspberry pi or other microcontroller platforms) in advance.
  • Participants are encouraged to speak to the coordinators after registration
